Approach

This ongoing weekend project gives users a platform to create campaigns for their own projects to receive contributions from wallet to wallet.

Motivation

Open source projects often face challenges in securing adequate funding. Line, an open source project itself, wants to change that. I was curious about the web3 space and this project gives me the opportunity to explore, learn and expand my horizon.

Features

  • Sign up via GitHub's Oauth flow
  • See all open repositories of which you are the owner
  • Add a wallet through the web3Modal of WalletConnect
  • Create or edit a campaign for each repo or your organization
  • WIP: Contribute to a project or organization
  • WIP: Overview of all contributions to your projects
  • WIP: Add bounties to projects and smart contracts
  • Home
  • Case study Visa calculator
